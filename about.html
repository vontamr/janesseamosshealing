What Makes Jane’s Seamoss Creations Different

At Jane’s Seamoss Creations, purity, sourcing, and intention are at the heart of everything we create. Our sea moss is carefully selected, thoughtfully prepared, and infused with nature — never shortcuts.

Wildcrafted Sea Moss from Antigua

Our sea moss is wildcrafted and sourced from the pristine waters of Antigua, an island known for its clean ocean environment and mineral-rich sea plants. We partner with trusted harvesters who practice sustainable, ethical collection methods to protect both the ocean and its ecosystems.

Every batch of sea moss is:

  • ✓ Thoroughly cleaned and hand-inspected
  • ✓ Slowly soaked in 100% distilled water (never tap water)
  • ✓ Prepared in small batches for quality and consistency

This intentional process allows us to deliver a clean, high-quality gel that retains its full spectrum of 92+ minerals.

Naturally Sweetened by Bees

Our infused sea moss gels are made with real fruit sourced from Colombia, where the fruit is naturally sweetened through traditional pollination by bees — not added sugars. The result is a smooth, flavorful gel that honors the natural sweetness of the fruit without compromising wellness.

What You’ll Never Find In Our Products:

  • ✕ No preservatives
  • ✕ No fillers
  • ✕ No artificial colors
  • ✕ No artificial flavors

Only pure sea moss, distilled water, and real fruit — that’s it.

🤍 Crafted with Integrity & Purpose

Jane’s Seamoss Creations was created with transparency, care, and wellness in mind. We believe you deserve to know where your sea moss comes from, how it’s processed, and what’s in it — because quality matters.
